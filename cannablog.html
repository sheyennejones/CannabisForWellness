In late 2018 I was diagnosed with Complex PTSD, Major Depressive Disorder, and Generalized Anxiety Disorder. I was suffering greatly on a daily basis. Work was becoming impossible to accomplish, I was getting no sleep because of my chronic nightmares, and my hyper vigilance was so severe that I always felt like I was on the run in a life or death situation. My suffering was visible to everyone around me, and eventually a good friend of mine asked if I wanted to smoke cannabis with her before I went to bed just to see if it might give me a little relief. That night was the first night in a very long time that I didn't constantly wake up from disturbing nightmares. It was about this same time that Utah legalized medical cannabis. I quickly found a doctor who was qualified to prescribe me a medical card, and now here I am 4 years later... Medicinal cannabis is a huge passion of mine. I wish for every human with PTSD to experience the safe relief that I have found, whether they find that through cannabis, traditional medications, therapy, or whatever else safely works for them.

DISCLAIMER: Any content on this website is not meant to be taken as professional medical advice. I am a web developer, not a doctor. :) This project is just for fun and to build my web dev portfolio. If you live in a state where medical cannabis is legal and you are seeking actual medical advice, consult with your doctor.
