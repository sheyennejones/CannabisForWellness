My suffering was visible to everyone around me, and eventually a good friend of mine asked if I wanted to smoke cannabis with her before I went to bed just to see if it might give me a little relief. I quickly found a doctor who was qualified to prescribe me a medical card, and now here I am 4 years later... Medicinal cannabis is a huge passion of mine.
